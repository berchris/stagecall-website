How it works

Simple for managers.
Effortless for crew.

Set it up once. StageCall handles the rest — from load-in to curtain up.

🎬
Step 01

Create a production

Add your show details, set up your teams — Sound, Lighting, Wardrobe, Stage Manager — and invite your crew by email or phone.

⏱
Step 02

Build your call schedule

Set up all your calls — "Half hour", "Orchestra warm-up", "Places" — with how many minutes before show each one fires.

🔔
Step 03

Run the show

Crew get alerts at the right moment. One acknowledgment clears the call for the entire team. No chasing, no repeating yourself.

Features

Everything your production needs

Designed for the realities of live theater — fast, reliable, and built for the chaos of tech week.

🎯

Team-specific alerts

Calls go only to the teams that need them. Sound doesn't get Wardrobe's alerts. Each team sees exactly their schedule, nothing more.

✅

Group acknowledgment

One tap from any team member clears the call for the whole team. No chasing everyone for individual confirmations.

📅

Full production timeline

See every call across all teams in chronological order. Know exactly where you are in the schedule at a glance.

👁

Team peek

Production managers and Stage Managers can view any team's call schedule in read-only mode without leaving the app.

📋

Alert history

Full log of every alert that fired — what was acknowledged, by whom, and when. Perfect for post-show review.

✏️

Live editing

Need to adjust a call mid-show? Managers can edit or delete calls on the fly. Changes reflect instantly for all crew.
